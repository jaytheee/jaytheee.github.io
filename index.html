favourite album (2025)

i felt like a sketch

by key vs. locket

key vs. locket is a massive inspiration to me and i’m glad to say that their new debut album is my favorite this year so far. with amazing tracks, particularly ‘snowman, snowman’ and ‘nothing for a moment’, i felt like this album deserved to be my favourite this year.

it has definitely been enjoyable to listen to during commutes and i recommend people to try it out!

more albums i love!

i didn't mean to haunt you

by quadeca

one of the most mesmerizing pieces of art from an unexpected artist. i didn't mean to haunt you introduced me to another side of music which i never really explored: story. althought its simple, being a ghost who died from suicide that starts to ponder on many things such as family and life, the execution of the idea is amazing and i abosolutely adore it.
