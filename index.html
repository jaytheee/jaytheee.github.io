favourite album
(of 2025)

i felt like a sketch

by key vs. locket

key vs. locket is a massive inspiration to me and i’m glad to say that their new debut album is my favorite this year so far. with amazing tracks, particularly ‘snowman, snowman’ and ‘nothing for a moment’, i felt like this album deserved to be number one for me this year.

it has definitely been enjoyable to listen to during commutes and i recommend a lot of people to listen to it!
